# 1G Ideas

## Document control

Objective: Maintain the authoritative registry of research ideas without
turning them into objectives before Gap Analysis.

Inputs: Draft ideas, meeting notes, emails, conceptual sketches, and
participant suggestions.

Outputs: One reusable idea record per idea.

Dependencies: `docs/phase1/1A_People.md` and the preceding Phase 1 records.

Completion criteria: Ideas are recorded once, with stable IDs, provenance, and
links to related people or evidence.

Version: 1.0

Status: Locked

Last update: 2026-07-01

## Scope guard

Do not infer scientific objectives in this document. Objectives can only be
formed after Phase 4 - Gap Analysis.
