# 1E Collaborations

## Document control

Objective: Maintain the authoritative registry of collaborations.

Inputs: Collaboration agreements, coauthorship networks, institutional
partnerships, international links, and meeting notes.

Outputs: One reusable collaboration record per collaboration.

Dependencies: `docs/phase1/1A_People.md`,
`docs/phase1/1B_Publications.md`, and
`docs/phase1/1C_PreviousProjects.md`.

Completion criteria: Collaborations are recorded once, with stable IDs and
links to related people, institutions, projects, and publications.
