# 1H Bibliography

## Document control

Objective: Maintain the authoritative bibliography for the research program.

Inputs: References from publications, CVs, project documents, reports, and
future proposal materials.

Outputs: One reusable bibliographic record per reference.

Dependencies: `docs/phase1/1B_Publications.md` and the preceding Phase 1
records.

Completion criteria: Bibliographic entries are recorded once, with stable IDs
and links to the documents that use them.
