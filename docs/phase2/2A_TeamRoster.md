## Scope guard

This subsection records who is in the Phase 2 team map and which institution
hosts each person. It does not rank institutions or assign institutional roles.

## Team overview

The current Phase 1A team contains sixteen people across six institutions:
UERJ, UFF, UFOB, SENAI CIMATEC, ORNL, and Universidade de Aveiro.

Academic merit fellowship information remains authoritative in
`docs/phase1/1A_People.md`. In summary, Phase 1A records identify CNPq
Productivity or FAPESB/CNPq Productivity fellowship status for six people,
FAPERJ Cientista do Nosso Estado for one person, and FAPERJ Jovem Cientista do
Nosso Estado or related young-scientist merit records for four people.
