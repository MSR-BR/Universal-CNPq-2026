## Document control

Objective: Synthesize the Phase 1 knowledge base into a reusable team book
and capability map.

Inputs: Phase 1 people, publications, previous projects, infrastructure,
collaborations, preliminary results, idea seeds, bibliography records, and
source inventory entries.

Outputs: One factual Team Book index with source-backed institutional roster,
person profiles, capability domains, and evidence links for future analytical
phases, plus Phase 2 subsection documents.

Dependencies: `docs/phase1/1A_People.md`,
`docs/phase1/1B_Publications.md`,

`docs/phase1/1G_Ideas.md`, and
`docs/phase1/1H_Bibliography.md`.

Completion criteria: Every Phase 1A participant is represented once; capability
domains are linked to Phase 1 evidence; no scientific objectives, gaps,
work packages, proposal text, or team responsibilities are created.

Last update: 2026-07-02

## Scope guard

This document is only a Team Book and capability map.

Allowed work in Phase 2:

- summarize Phase 1A people records;
- group source-backed capabilities by theme;
- connect people, institutions, publications, projects, infrastructure,
  collaborations, preliminary results, idea seeds, and bibliography records;
- preserve academic merit fellowship information already recorded in Phase 1A.

Still prohibited:

- do not infer scientific objectives;
- do not analyse gaps;
- do not rank people, institutions, or themes;
- do not assign responsibilities;
- do not create work packages;
- do not write CNPq proposal text.
