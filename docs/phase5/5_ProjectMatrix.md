## Matrix design principle

Metal complexes are the aggregating element of the project. The project matrix
therefore treats metal-complex systems as the common scientific platform across
experimental synthesis/characterization, pressure tuning, quantum
thermodynamics, quantum batteries, and quantum-resource analysis.

Matrix entries preserve three links:

1. the metal-complex material basis;
2. the experimental or computational variable being studied;
3. the evidence, capability, and validation route that supports later project
   design.
